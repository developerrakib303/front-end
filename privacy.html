At Haya BD, we believe great fashion should never come at the cost of your privacy. This Policy explains what information we collect when you browse and shop with us, how we use it, and the choices and rights you have over your personal data.

01 Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Haya BD ("Haya BD", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, stores and protects the personal information of customers and visitors who use our website, mobile experience and related services (together, the "Services"). It applies to everyone who interacts with Haya BD, whether you place an order, create an account, or simply browse our collections.

By accessing or using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy. We operate from Dhaka, Bangladesh, and handle your data in line with applicable Bangladeshi laws, including the principles of the Digital Security Act and consumer-protection regulations.

02 Information We Collect

We only collect information that helps us fulfil your orders, improve your experience and keep your account secure. The data we collect falls into the following categories:

Information you provide directly

  • Account details — your name, email address, mobile number and password when you register.
  • Order & delivery details — billing and shipping addresses, recipient name and contact number.
  • Payment information — your chosen payment method; card and mobile-wallet details are entered directly with our payment partners and are not stored on our servers.
  • Communications — messages, reviews, support tickets and feedback you send us.

Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage data — IP address, browser type, device identifiers and the pages you view.
  • Shopping behaviour — items viewed, added to cart, wishlisted and purchased.
  • Cookies & similar technologies — as described in the Cookies & Tracking section below.

03 How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information for clearly defined, legitimate purposes, including to:

  1. Process, fulfil and deliver your orders, and send you order confirmations and shipping updates.
  2. Create and manage your account, and provide customer support.
  3. Handle returns, exchanges, refunds and warranty requests.
  4. Personalise your shopping experience with relevant products and recommendations.
  5. Send you marketing communications about new arrivals, offers and promotions — only where you have opted in.
  6. Detect and prevent fraud, abuse and other unlawful activity.
  7. Comply with our legal and regulatory obligations.

We will never sell your personal data. You can unsubscribe from marketing messages at any time using the link in any email or by contacting us directly.

04 Cookies & Tracking

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help our site work and remember your preferences. We use them to keep you signed in, hold the contents of your cart, and understand how our store is used so we can improve it.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies — required for core features such as login, checkout and security. The site will not function correctly without them.
  • Performance & analytics cookies — help us measure traffic and improve page speed and content.
  • Marketing cookies — let us show you more relevant offers on our site and on third-party platforms.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent parts of the checkout and account areas from working as intended.

05 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share your information only when it is necessary to run our store and serve you, and always under appropriate safeguards. We may share data with:

  • Delivery partners — couriers and logistics providers who need your address and contact number to deliver your order.
  • Payment processors — trusted gateways (including bKash, Nagad and card networks) to securely complete your transactions.
  • Technology providers — hosting, analytics and customer-support tools that operate on our behalf.
  • Authorities — where required by law, court order or to protect our rights and the safety of others.

All third parties are required to protect your data and may only use it for the purposes we specify. We do not permit them to use your information for their own marketing.

06 Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously and apply industry-standard safeguards to protect it. These include encrypted connections (SSL/TLS), restricted internal access, secure payment handling through certified partners, and regular reviews of our systems.

While we work hard to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure. We encourage you to choose a strong, unique password and to keep your account credentials confidential. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

07 Your Rights

You have meaningful control over your personal information. Subject to applicable law, you may:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any information that is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Delete your account and associated data, where we are not required to retain it.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in the Contact Us section. We will respond within a reasonable period and may ask you to verify your identity before acting on your request.

08 Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for adults. While we proudly sell clothing for children, all purchases must be made by a parent, guardian or adult aged 18 or over. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal data without parental consent, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to remove that information from our records.

09 Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you by email or through a notice on our website.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of our Services after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

10 Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, we would be glad to help.
